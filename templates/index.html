About FloodGuard SEA

FloodGuard SEA is a comprehensive flood early warning system that combines two complementary AI approaches to provide accurate and timely flood predictions for Southeast Asian regions.

Subsystem A (LSTM + Fuzzy Logic) analyzes weather patterns and rainfall data to forecast future precipitation and assess flood risk using intelligent fuzzy inference systems.

Subsystem B (CNN) processes satellite imagery to detect existing flood conditions, providing ground-truth validation and real-time situational awareness.

Together, these subsystems offer a multi-modal approach to flood monitoring and prediction, combining predictive modeling with observational data for enhanced accuracy and reliability.
